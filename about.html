About Nexus AI

We're building the first community-owned artificial general intelligence, built on the shoulders of open research.

Our Foundation

Nexus AI is built on the pioneering work of Andrej Karpathy and his autoresearch project. Autoresearch demonstrated that AI agents can autonomously discover, train, and evaluate models—creating a self-improving loop of artificial intelligence development.

"The vision is a distributed network where anyone can contribute compute and be rewarded for advancing AI capabilities." — Andrej Karpathy

We believe this vision should be community-owned. That's why we built Nexus AI on top of these open foundations—not to replace them, but to extend them into a decentralized, fair, and democratic AI infrastructure.

What We Build

While autoresearch showed what's possible with autonomous AI research, Nexus AI adds:

🌐 Decentralization

Anyone with a computer can contribute and earn tokens. No central authority.

💰 Economic Layer

$NEXUS token rewards contributors fairly. Staking gives governance rights.

🗳️ Community Governance

Token holders vote on research priorities, fund allocation, and upgrades.

⚡ Real-Time Streaming

Superfluid integration means you get paid continuously, not in batches.

Our Mission

To democratize artificial general intelligence.

AI is the most transformative technology humanity has ever created. It should be owned by everyone, not just a handful of corporations. Nexus AI is our attempt to make that vision real.

We believe in:

  • Open source over closed AI
  • Community ownership over corporate control
  • Fair compensation over extraction
  • Decentralization over centralization

Team

Nexus AI is an open project. There's no traditional team—we're a community of researchers, developers, and enthusiasts building the future of AI together.

Core contributors are funded by the Treasury allocation (15% of $NEXUS tokens), with decisions made by token holders through on-chain governance.
