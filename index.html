How It Works

A decentralized network where anyone can contribute compute and earn rewards for building AGI.

1

Run the Agent

Download and run our auto-detecting agent on your computer. It automatically detects your hardware—whether GPU or CPU—and registers with the network.

2

Contribute Compute

Your machine runs AI tasks: inference, training LoRA adapters, or processing data. The coordinator routes work to available agents based on capability.

3

Earn $NEXUS

Get paid in $NEXUS tokens for every task completed. Stake your tokens for governance rights and exclusive rewards.

4

Own the Network

As a token holder, you own a piece of Nexus AI. Vote on development priorities and shape the future of AGI.
